How It Works

Nothing is stored on a server. All picks are encoded directly into the URL after the #. Friends send you their link, you paste them all into the Admin tab, and the site scores them live as you enter actual draft results.

For you (the commissioner):

  1. Open the Setup tab. Reorder the 32 teams to match the real 2026 draft order (including trades).
  2. (Optional) Paste a top-prospects list so friends get autocomplete suggestions.
  3. Click "Generate Config Link" and copy it. This is the link you send to your friends.
  4. During the draft, open the Admin tab. Paste friends' submitted links into the top textarea and hit "Load Entries".
  5. As each real pick happens, type the player name into the "Actual Results" row for that pick. Scoreboard updates live.

For your friends:

  1. They open the link you sent.
  2. On the Make Picks tab, they type their name and 32 player picks.
  3. They click "Generate My Picks Link", copy it, and text/DM it back to you.

Scoring

  • 5 points — exact pick (right player at right slot)
  • 3 points — within 3 picks (e.g. you had him at #25, he went #22)
  • 2 points — within 5 picks
  • 1 point — player picked anywhere in round 1

Hosting on GitHub Pages (free)

  1. Sign up / log in at github.com (free).
  2. Create a new public repo, e.g. nfl-mock-pickem.
  3. Upload this index.html to the repo (drag-and-drop works).
  4. In the repo: Settings → Pages → Source: "Deploy from branch" → Branch: main / root → Save.
  5. In about a minute, your site is live at https://<yourusername>.github.io/nfl-mock-pickem/.
  6. Send that URL to your friends.

No hosting fees, ever. Works on phones. No accounts needed for friends.
