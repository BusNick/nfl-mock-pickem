How It Works

Nothing is stored on a server. All picks are encoded directly into the URL after the #. Friends send you their link, you paste them all into the Admin tab, and the site scores them live as you enter actual draft results.

For you (the commissioner):

  1. Open the Setup tab. Adjust the team order and prospect pool if needed.
  2. Click "Generate Config Link" and copy it. This is the link you send to your friends. (Or just send them the plain site URL — defaults are already good.)
  3. During the draft, open the Admin tab. Paste friends' submitted links into the top textarea and hit "Load Entries".
  4. As each real pick happens, type the player name into the "Actual Results" row for that pick. Scoreboard updates live.

For your friends:

  1. They open the link you sent.
  2. On the Make Picks tab, they type their name and pick 32 players from the dropdowns.
  3. They click "Generate My Picks Link", copy it, and text/DM it back to you.

Scoring

  • 5 points — exact pick (right player at right slot)
  • 3 points — within 3 picks (e.g. you had him at #25, he went #22)
  • 2 points — within 5 picks
  • 1 point — player picked anywhere in round 1
